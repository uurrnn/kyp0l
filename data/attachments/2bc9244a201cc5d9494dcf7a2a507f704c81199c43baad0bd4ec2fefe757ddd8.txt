(1)  Any  person  who  fails  to  comply  with  any  provision  of  this  chapter,  any 

administrative  regulation  promulgated  under  this  chapter,  or  any  valid order  of 

the board shall be fined a reasonable amount as determined by the board through 

the promulgation of administrative regulations in accordance with KRS Chapter 

13A. Each day of violation or noncompliance shall constitute a separate offense. 

(2)  The  board  shall  assess  a  civil  penalty  against  an  individual  or  licensee  who 

performs diagnostic or therapeutic procedures without a valid license or aids or 

abets unlicensed or unlawful practice. 

(3)  Prior to assessing a civil penalty, the board shall confirm a violation by any of the 

following: 

(a)  Observing the violation; 

(b)  Obtaining records, documents, or other physical evidence; 

(c)  Obtaining statements that confirm the existence of the violation from either 

the employer or the employee; or 

(d)  Obtaining  statements  that  corroborate  the  allegation  that  a  violation  has 

occurred from a third party, such as a patient or coworker. 

(4)  A civil penalty shall be assessed against an individual who performs diagnostic or 

therapeutic procedures without a valid license as follows: 

(a)  Failure to apply for an initial license by an individual who is fully qualified

the license application fee;
