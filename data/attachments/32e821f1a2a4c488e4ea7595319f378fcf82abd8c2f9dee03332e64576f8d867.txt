Hilliard Musselman 
Hilliard Musselman Update 
Erin Wagoner, MSD Community Benefits and Partnerships 

01:09:16 
Erin Wagoner stated that drainage issues in the right-of-way area on South 
Ewing Road have contributed to downstream flow, which has likely contributed to erosion 
on the Hilliard Musselman property. Wagoner noted that MSD had previously completed 
a survey and determined that they could provide drainage improvement work within the 
MSD  right-of-way.  Wagoner  mentioned  that  once  a  contractor  has  been  selected  to 
complete the project, it would take roughly 2-3 months to finish. Noel Rueff will contact 
the current property owner, Bill Crawford, to discuss this work. Wagoner responded to 
questions from Board Members (see recording for details).
