1. 

O-058-26     AN ORDINANCE RELATING TO THE ZONING AND GENERAL DISTRICT 
DEVELOPMENT PLAN OF PROPERTIES LOCATED AT 12302, 12312, 12400, 12404 
AND 12406 TAYLORSVILLE ROAD AND 3800 AND 3830 SWEENEY LANE 
CONTAINING APPROXIMATELY 22.6 ACRES AND BEING IN LOUISVILLE METRO 
(CASE NO. 25ZONE0099). 

4/14/26 Planning and Zoning Committee  Recommended for Approval 
3/17/26 Planning and Zoning Committee  Held 
Action Required By:  May 20, 2026 

Sponsors: Andrew Owen (D-9)  

A motion was made by Committee Member Ruhe, seconded by Committee Member 
Benson, that this Ordinance be recommended for approval. 

Julia Williams, Office of Planning, gave a presentation on the Planning Commission’s 
recommendation (see attached). 

The following also spoke to this item: 
- Chair Owen  
- Committee Member Benson 
- Committee Member Parrish-Wright 
- Travis Fiechter, Jefferson County Attorney’s Office 

Travis Fiechter, Jefferson County Attorney’s Office, read a proposed amendment into 
the record. 

A motion was made by Committee Member Benson, seconded by Committee Member 
Ruhe, that this Ordinance be amended as shown in the attached document titled “O-
058-26 PROPOSED CABS 041426 Zoning and General District Development Plan at 
12302, 12312, 12400, & 12406 Taylorsville Rd & 3800 & 3830 Sweeney Ln.pdf.” 

Chair Owen spoke to the proposed amendment and explained that a YES vote for 
the amendment by substitution would reject the Planning Commission’s 
recommendation. 

The motion to amend failed by the following vote: 

Yes: 2 – Benson (R-20), Ruhe (D-21)  
No: 3 – Owen (D-9), Reed (R-16), Parrish-Wright (D-3) 
Present: 1 – Raymond (D-10) 
Excused: 2 – Hudson (R-23), Batshon (R-25) 

The discussion continued and the following spoke to this item: 
- Committee Member Raymond 
- Chair Owen 
- Travis Fiechter 
- Committee Member Benson
