Pending Legislation 

1. 

O-032-26     AN ORDINANCE RELATING TO THE ZONING OF PROPERTY 
LOCATED AT 5417 VALLEY STATION ROAD CONTAINING APPROXIMATELY 0.38 
ACRES AND BEING IN LOUISVILLE METRO (CASE NO. 25ZONE0024). (AS 
AMENDED) 

3/17/26 Planning and Zoning Committee  Amended; Recommended for Approval 
3/3/26 Planning and Zoning Committee  Held 
2/17/26 Planning and Zoning Committee  Tabled 
Action Required By:  April 15, 2026 

Sponsors: Andrew Owen (D-9)  

A motion was made by Committee Member Ruhe, seconded by Committee Member 
Benson, that this Ordinance be recommended for approval. 

Brian Davis, Office of Planning, spoke to this item. 

Chair Owen stated that he had spoken to Committee Member Batshon and that he is in 
support of this zoning item. 

Travis Fiechter, Jefferson County Attorney’s Office, read a proposed amendment to 
Binding Element #6, and newly proposed Binding Elements #7, #8, #9, #10, and #11 
into the record. 

A motion was made by Committee Member Ruhe, seconded by Committee Member 
Raymond, that this Ordinance be amended by amending Binding Element #6 and 
adding Binding Elements #7, #8, #9, #10, and #11, as shown in the attached document 
titled “O-032-26 V.2 CAM 031726 Zoning at 5417 Valley Station Road.pdf.” 

The motion to amend carried by a voice vote. 

The motion to recommend for approval carried by the following vote and the amended 
Ordinance was sent to Old Business: 

Yes: 5 – Owen (D-9), Reed (R-16), Raymond (D-10), Benson (R-20), Ruhe (D-21)  
Absent: 1 – Parrish-Wright (D-3) 
Excused: 2 – Hudson (R-23), Batshon (R-25)
