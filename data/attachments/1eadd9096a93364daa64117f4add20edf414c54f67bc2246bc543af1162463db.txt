Special Discussion 

ID 26-0034     Metro Council’s Legislation Software – PrimeGov vs. Legistar 

Sonya Harward, Metro Council Clerk, briefly spoke about the on-going discussion the Council has 
had for a few months regarding changing the Council’s legislative management system from the 
PrimeGov system to Legistar system, which was used previously by the Council.   

A motion was made by Council Member Piagentini, seconded by Council Memer Batshon, that the 
change to the Legistar system be approved.  The motion carried by a voice vote.
