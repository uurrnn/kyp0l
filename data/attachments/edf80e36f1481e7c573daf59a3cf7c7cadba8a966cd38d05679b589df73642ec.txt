adversely affect the public health, safety, or welfare, or cause a hazard or nuisance to the 
public as the proposed structure will be constructed in accordance with applicable building 
codes to ensure safety and structural integrity, and  

WHEREAS, the Board of Zoning Adjustment finds, the requested variance will not alter 
the  essential  character  of  the  general  vicinity  and  will  not  allow  an  unreasonable 
circumvention of the requirements of the zoning regulations as the general area follows 
the  Traditional  Neighborhood  form  district  pattern  of  development.  The  Traditional 
Neighborhood  Form  District  intends  to  promote  development  and  redevelopment 
consistent  and  compatible  with  the  distinct  site  and  community  design  elements  of  a 
traditional  neighborhood  according  to  the  Land  Development  Code.  The  proposed 
principal structure follows the same setbacks as the homes in the general vicinity and will 
maintain the 4 components of Traditional Neighborhood residential site design. The site 
is subject to infill requirements for building height, which states that the building height 
shall fall within the range of building heights of existing structures within the same block 
face. The only reason this variance is being requested is because the subject site is zoned 
EZ-1 and must follow the side yard setback requirements set by the Land Development 
Code, otherwise, the proposed home will be like the homes in the general vicinity, and  

WHEREAS, the Board of Zoning Adjustment finds, the requested variance does not arise 
from circumstances which do not generally apply to land in the general vicinity or in the 
same zone, because this lot is similar in size and shape to other lots in the surrounding 
area, which also feature similar setbacks, and
