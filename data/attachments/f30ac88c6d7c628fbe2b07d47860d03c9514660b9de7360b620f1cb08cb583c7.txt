WHEREAS, the Board of Zoning Adjustment finds that the proposed nonconforming use, 
a bar/tavern, is allowed in a less restrictive classification than the current nonconforming 
use of adult entertainment establishments. Adult entertainment establishments are only 
permitted by right in M-3, the most intensive industrial zoning district. Industrial zoning 
allows  a  broader  and  more  intense  range  of  uses.  Bars  are  permitted  by  right  in  C-2 
Commercial. Commercial zoning districts restrict industrial uses, and  

WHEREAS, the Board of Zoning Adjustment finds that the new nonconforming use would 
be no more odious or offensive to surrounding properties. Current land use regulations 
require that adult entertainment establishment’s public entrance not be located within 500 
feet of any property used for residential properties. This site is not required to maintain 
that separation requirement. By changing the non-conforming use, the site would lose it 
ability to operate adult entertainment activities near residential properties, now, therefore 
be it.
